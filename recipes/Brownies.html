Brownies

Descripiton

These are my grandmas homemade coconut brownie bars. Absolutly delicious and so easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 18 graham crackers (or 9 doubles), crushed into fine crumbs
  • 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (6-ounce) package chocolate "bits" (or chips)
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts

    • Directions

    1. Combine all ingrediants in mixing bowl until fully coated.
    2. Grease and line a 9 inch square baking dish.
    3. Add the mixture and use a spatula to ensure it's even inside the pan
    4. Bake in 350 F oven for 30-40 min checking when you can smell them in the oven
    5. Cut into small squares, Serve and enjoy!
