Banana Bread

Description

This banana bread recipe is simply the greatest I have come accross in all my time creating recipes

It is using fresh and spoiled bananas and is a great way to use up that pesky produce that you dont want to waste

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 ⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F and lightly grease a 9x5 loaf pan.
  2. combine flour bakind soda and salt in bowl and beat brown sugar and butter in a seperate bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas. Combine into flour mixture until just combined.
  3. Pour batter into greased loaf pan
  4. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted comes out clean, about 60 min.
  5. Let bread cool in pan for 10 min and then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely
  6. Enjoy!
